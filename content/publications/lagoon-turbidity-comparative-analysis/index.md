abstract: |
  This study presents a comprehensive comparative analysis of turbidity dynamics
  in two contrasting lagoon systems -- Xincun and Li'an Lagoons in Hainan, China -- over
  the period 2019-2025. Using Normalized Difference Turbidity Index (NDTI) derived
  from Google Earth Engine, we examine how differences in lagoon morphology and
  riverine input modulate spatial and temporal turbidity patterns. Special attention
  is given to extreme weather events, including typhoons, and their short-term vs.
  long-term impacts on water clarity.
